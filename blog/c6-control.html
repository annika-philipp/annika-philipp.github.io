Control

When programming, what are some of my triggers? What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

At this stage it is either getting stuck when not understanding what I'm being asked to do (mainly resulting from not understanding the lingo), when I understand the question but cannot figure out what to do or when I think I have finally figured it out and ... nada.

That being said, I wouldn't say it causes a disproportionate emotional response. I'm only 3 weeks into this course without any previous experience, so I'm not surprised by running into roadblocks. I mean this is quite a normal learning process/experience so I'm not too worried.

How did I find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

I expect this will be a really handy tool once we hit bootcamp. Especially the breathing part is good advice. And just to be a nerd: breathing calmly calms the amygdala (because you can't breath calmly when you're amygdala is panicking), and a calm brain is the perfect basis for creative thinking, engagement and learning. So yeah, deep breaths.
