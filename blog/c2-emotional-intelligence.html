What learnings did I take from Search Inside Yourself?

All of the topics mentioned in this chapter have been part of my professional work over the last few years, so emotional intelligence, neuroplasticity/neurology, leadership and mindfulness aren’t new topics for me. I liked how the author highlighted the fact that emotional intelligence is not an innate ability but a learned skill (although some people tend to naturally already have a greater awareness of this than others). On a personal level, having been exposed to these theories for quite some time, I am reminded of how emotional intelligence is an ongoing project, sometimes I am better at managing my emotions than at other times, sometimes my awareness of stress (or equally happiness) factors is better tuned than others.

Two minutes of mindfulness

This is something I’ve struggled with in the past, but in the spirit of making tiny changes, something I am going to give a shot for the next wee while. I’ve always found running and solely focussing on my breath easier than sitting still and calming my thoughts. 2 minutes should be doable though…

Clearly work in progress!
