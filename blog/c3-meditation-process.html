Meditation

How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way I tackled the site redesign and rebuild

It had a huge impact on how I approached this challenge. For a start I was confident that this week's learning had given me the tools and resources to do this redesign/rebuild. I started this project in a very structured way, spending quite a bit of time thinking and hand drawing/writing before actually coding. I kept repeating ‘process over product’ like a mantra in my head, and it really helped me to focus on what was important and not get carried away choosing colours, fonts, font sizes, the perfect sizing etc.

Does this website look exactly like 'perfectionist-me' would like it to look like? No!
Am I a graphic designer? No!
So does it matter for this week's task? No!
Did I achieve the functionality and layout I had in mind though? Yes...mostly!

What did I think about meditation before reading chapter 2

I have researched and used the concept of meditation a lot in my current job so know a fair bit about its effects on the brain and people's lives in general. However, I've always struggled to meditate myself, probably because I like to be active, am always busy and get a lot of wandering thoughts when I do try.

What new things have I learnt about meditation?

That I can be okay with wandering thoughts. I am training my brain each time I become aware that my mind wanders and then refocus my attention. So in the spirit of process over product, I'll persevere with meditation, starting with just a few minutes, but aiming to eventually increase this.
