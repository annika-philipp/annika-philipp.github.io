What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Self awareness is the same as mindfulness in the sense that both allow us to reflect on or take note off our emotions, mental and/or physical state in the present moment. Both are done in a non-judgemental/neutral way and both are learnable and definitely get easier the more we practice.

I did the Journaling for Self-Assement with the prompts suggested in the book. This was a fairly easy task although I surprised myself with some of the things I came up with especially when there was still time left and I started to reflect a lot deeper.

What did I take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

That emotion are what I feel not who I am. I imagine that saying 'I feel stressed' instead of saying 'I am stressed' could also in the long run have positive effects on how my body copes with stress.
